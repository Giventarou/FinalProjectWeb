WHY I LOVE ANIME

Anime is more than just entertainment for me. It's a source of inspiration, motivation, and life lessons. Every character has their own struggles that relate to real life.

What I love most about anime is how they teach us about friendship, hard work, and never giving up. Especially shounen anime that always motivates me to chase my dreams!

Besides watching, I also love drawing anime characters. It's my way of expressing creativity and appreciating the beautiful art that anime offers.
